What is an GUID?

If you're new to CPAN Testers, then you may not be aware of what a GUID is. When CPAN Testers reports are submitted to the Metabase, they are given a GUID. This uniquely identifies the report within the system. If you have received a Daily Report, the GUID is the alphanumeric part at the end of each report link.

Examples below highlight the GUID in URLs you are likely to see:
